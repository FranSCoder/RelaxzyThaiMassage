Traditional thai massage

The Traditional Thai massage, globally renowned for its countless benefits, is based on the use of ancient techniques of passive stretching and localized, controlled pressure to relieve muscle tension and balance blocked energies, promoting both physical and mental calm.

It is performed in appropriate attire provided by us, you can change in our designated changing rooms. No oils are used, it begins with a warm foot bath to improve blood circulation.
